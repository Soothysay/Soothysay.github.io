About Me

I am a PhD student of Computer Science at The University of Iowa advised by Dr. Bijaya Adhikari. Research Interest

My primary interests lie in the intersection of Machine Learning and Deep Learning with applications in Finance and Healthcare. Currently, I am exploring the domain of Epidemiological Modelling and am trying to also learn Graph Neural Networks and Graph Convolutional Networks. My primary programming language is Python and I have good knowledge of Tensorflow and PySpark. I also have a fair bit of experience in model risk validation for Financial Institutions during my stint as a Consultant at Solytics Partners LTD
