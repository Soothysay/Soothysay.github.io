Machine learning · Healthcare · Uncertainty

Akash Choudhuri

PhD Candidate in Computer Science

University of Iowa

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Iowa, advised by Prof. Bijaya Adhikari. My research develops reliable machine learning methods for healthcare, with an emphasis on hypergraphs, clinical risk prediction, and distribution-free uncertainty quantification.

I am part of the Computational Epidemiology Research Group and the AlgoEpi Reading Group. Before graduate school, I worked on model-risk validation for financial institutions at Solytics Partners.
